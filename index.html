COVID-19 and Google Trends Visualization

Visualizations by Diya Nair

Objective

The current COVID-19 pandemic is one of the largest challenges society has faced in the 21st century. The US has been in various forms of lockdown since the middle of March. The only way out seems to be when a vaccine is available to the public.

The public health officials need up to date information on the COVID-19 infection and death rates which they use to determine the “phases” of lockdown. The problem with COVID-19 data is that there is a lag between when a person tests positive versus when they are asymptomatically transmissive.

Google trends are a great way to measure society’s (mostly) honest view on popular topics and challenges. This project is an attempt to analyze Google trend data to see if there is a correlation between google searches and future COVID-19 hotspots.
